# Moonlight 

[Moonlight](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.limelight) streams games, programs, or your full desktop from an NVIDIA GameStream-compatible PC on your local network or the Internet using NVIDIA GeForce Experience.

If your using an AMD gpu then install [Sunshine](https://github.com/loki-47-6F-64/sunshine/releases) on your PC instead.

# Stadia

Stadia is a cloud gaming service developed and operated by Google. It works perfectly fine on the Retroid Pocket 2 Plus. Be mindful of the missing buttons when selecting games (L3/R3 namely).

# Steam Link

Steam Link is a software solution made by Valve Corporation for streaming Steam content from a personal computer or Steam Machine wirelessly to a mobile device. 

If you're having issues streaming to your Retroid Pocket 2 Plus (such as instant disconnection) try enabling HEVC Video.

<details>
<summary>Touch Overlay (Add/Remove Buttons)</summary>
<br>

You can use the touch screen overlay to add buttons (such as the missing L3/R3).

1. Settings > Controller > Enable Touch Controller
2. In-Game touch the `...` on the top left > touch `Layout Controls` > Touch `Edit Layout`
3. Drag and Drop with the touchscreen to remove and add buttons. 
4. Touch the `...` again and touch `x` when finished
