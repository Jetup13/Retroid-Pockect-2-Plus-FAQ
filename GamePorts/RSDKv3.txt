Open this link: https://github.com/Rubberduckycooly/Sonic-CD-11-Decompilation/actions

Click the latest workflow event

Click or scroll down to Artifacts

Download v3-Android.zip

Extract the apk called `app-debug.apk` from the zip

Install the apk on your device (Google Play might flag apk, ignore this)

Run RSDKv3 and give permissions once

Locate the folder `/RSDK/v3/` on your internal storage

Place your Sonic CD `Data.rsdk` in the /RSDK/v3/

Run RSDKv3 and enjoy!
