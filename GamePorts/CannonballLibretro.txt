First download the core and required content files

RetroArch Main Menu > Online Updater > Core Downloader > CannonBall (Outrun)

RetroArch Main Menu > Online Updater > Content Downloader > CannonBall > CannonBall.zip

Copy the required OutRun Revision B ROM files to your RetroArch downloads folder 

`Internal shared storage\RetroArch\downloads\CannonBall\`
This folder will also include a text file of which outrun files are needed

Load `CannonBall\CannonBall.game` from `Downloads` through Load Content on RetroArch
