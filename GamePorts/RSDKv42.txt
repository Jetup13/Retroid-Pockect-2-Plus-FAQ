Open this link: https://github.com/Rubberduckycooly/Sonic-1-2-2013-Decompilation/actions

Click the latest workflow event

Click or scroll down to Artifacts

Download v4-Android.zip

Extract the apk called `app-debug.apk` from the zip

Install the apk on your device (Google Play might flag apk, ignore this)

Run RSDKv4 and give permissions once

Locate the folder `/RSDK/v4/` on your internal storage

Place your Sonic 2 `Data.rsdk` in the /RSDK/v4/

Run RSDKv4 and enjoy!

(Note you can only have one game (Sonic 1 or Sonic 2) installed at a time)
